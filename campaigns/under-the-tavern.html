Living Timeline



The living timeline was created to organize multiple 5E homebrew Dungeons and Dragons campaigns in the same universe. Since this is a continuous "world" with various campaigns, player choices drastically affect the overall world, pantheons, settlements, and NPCs.

The world's timeline is divided into different periods and documented by major events. Most time periods represent a new campgain starting inside the world. Events are ogranized by the color of icon as follows:

TIMELINE COLOR KEY


» GREEN → A successful outcome

» YELLOW → A moderate or okay outcome

» RED → A bad or negative outcome

» DARK GREEN → A character joins the party

» BLACK → A player character dies or a total party wipe

» BLUE → A stand-alone one-shot campaign

» PURPLE → A player unaffected backstory event
