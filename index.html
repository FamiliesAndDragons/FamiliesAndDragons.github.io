Languages Rework

Players hate missing critical information when their characters can't read something in-game, and you can't always have the comprehend languages spell prepared. On the other hand, ask any DM, and they'll let you know handwritten calligraphy in made-up fonts can be a headache.
The purpose of the rework was two-fold, to make it easier for players and DMs alike.
