World Timeline



The world timeline was created to organize multiple 5E homebrew Dungeons and Dragons campaigns in the same universe. Since this is a continuous "world" with various campaigns, player choices drastically affect the overall world, pantheons, settlements, and NPCs.

The world's timeline is divided into different periods and documented by major events. Most time periods represent a new campgain starting inside the world. Events are ogranized by the color of icon and time period.

  • After The Great War

    AGW

    As the world rebuilds, the understanding of science and arcane arts expands rapidly. While most factions focus on using this newfound understanding for the benefits of civilization and creating solutions, one faction has its eyes set on a very different type of goal. The White Scales Empire uses experimental science and arcane advancement to achieve ultimate power, regardless of how great the sacrifices may be.
