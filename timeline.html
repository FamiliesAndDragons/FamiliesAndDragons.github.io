World Timeline



The world timeline was created to organize multiple 5E homebrew Dungeons and Dragons campaigns in the same universe. Since this is a continuous "world" with various campaigns, player choices drastically affect the overall world, pantheons, settlements, and NPCs.

The world's timeline is divided into different periods and documented by major events. Most time periods represent a new campgain starting inside the world. Events are ogranized by the color of icon and time period as follows:

TIMELINE COLOR KEY


» PINK → A campaign

» BLUE → A stand-alone one-shot campaign

» DARK BLUE → A player affected outcome

» PURPLE → A player unaffected backstory event
