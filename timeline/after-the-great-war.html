After the Great War



As the world rebuilds, the understanding of science and arcane arts expands rapidly. The constant research leads to various discoveries and re-discoveries of different use cases and solutions to modern problems.

While most factions focus on using this newfound understanding for the benefits of civilization and creating solutions, one faction has its eyes set on a very different type of goal. The White Scales Empire uses experimental science and arcane advancement to achieve ultimate power, regardless of how great the sacrifices may be.

  • The Party is Formed

    AGW

    A group of adventurers takes a job from a bulletin board in Phandilan to escort a supply caravan headed south. Shortly after leaving town, the group is ambushed by a White Scales Empire Raiding Party. After neutralizing the threats, the adventures realize that these aren't ordinary supply carts. Instead, they were hauling munitions, armor, and weapons. The adventurers chase after the last cart that scurried away before the fight broke out.

  • We were setup!

    AGW

    The group caught up to the last supply car, but they were too late. The cart is set ablaze in the middle of the road, and the drivers are beheaded. Upon inspecting the cart, Fhakir and his guard emerge from the brush and surprise the adventurers. Fhakir makes quick work of the group and leaves them beaten and badly wounded. The adventurers fall unconscious from their battle.

  • Khurrat, The Shady Market

    AGW

    The adventurers suddenly regain consciousness and spring to their feet from under a pile of bodies and corpses; To everyone’s surprise, the party was very much alive. After their battle, a local vendor collected their bodies assuming they were dead in an attempt to sell them as supplies for dark arcane arts.

  • The Sanguine Rebellion

    AGW

    The group begins asking around town about the rebel group known as the Sanguine Rebellion. As the group asks questions around the docks, they are suddenly abducted and blindfolded. Their captor, The Red Maiden, leader of the Sanguine Rebellion, begins interrogating the group about their motives. However, after the adventurers show their battle scars from the previous encounter with Fhakir, The Red Maiden releases them from their bindings and begins a more civil line of questioning.

  • Clearing Out The Bandits

    AGW

    The Red Maiden propositions the group to aid her in exchange for her favor. Bandits have been flying a false flag and pretending to be the Sanguine Rebellion while robbing passing caravans. The adventurers travel to the bandit hideout in a cave, defeat everyone inside, and loot some treasure. When they return to the Red Maiden, she is thrilled with their work and provides them with an emblem bearing the crest of the Sanguine Rebellion.

  • Missing Marketing Materials

    AGW

    Hearing rumors of stray undead wandering in the nearby forest, the party investigates. After further inspection, the undead horde belongs to Chuck, a cultist of Smile Isle. Chuck asks the adventures to help him retrieve his lost marketing materials so he can continue his community outreach. Backtracking Chuck's steps, the adventurers find the marketing pamphlets and return them to the rightful owner. Before leaving, the party takes copies of the Smile Isle Outreach pamphlets and ask Chuck for more details on how to travel there.

  • Stopping by Visco City

    AGW

    The group traverses through the thick swamps and miserable terrain between Khuratt and Visco City. After two days of travel and fending off the wildlife, they eventually stumble upon the small settlement. The group secures a ride on a Smile Isle Trade Ship with the note from Chuck. The adventurers befriend the crew of the Kharon while they sail to their destination.

  • Service for Smile Isle

    AGW

    Upon arriving at Smile Isle, the adventurers explore and enjoy the resort's attractions. Later on, they seek an audience with the leader of Smile Isle, Alannn, who offers to pay the group handsomely to help by looking into the wildlife terrorizing the town of Vol' Dun. Alannn stresses the importance of ensuring Vol' Dun doesn't have to stop production and operations continue round the clock.

  • A Lucky Chance Encounter

    AGW

    Traveling through the arid wastelands of Smile Isle, the group successfully navigates through the windy path as evening sets. However, due to the harsh travels, the group decides to stop and rest for the evening. While Yui is watching through the night, she hears something moaning and struggling in the distance. To her shock, Yui discovers an undead laborer stuck in some rough foliage. After freeing the laborer, Yui decides to have the party follow him since he might know the best path through the rugged terrain ahead. Trusting her gut, Yui safely follows the undead laborer as it returns to Vol' Dun, avoiding the hardships average travelers face.

  • Returning to Smile City

    AGW

    Guards at the gates of Smile City escort the group directly to the castle to speak with Alannn about the urgent news. Alannn quickly explains his worries surrounding the appearance of a Chimera before begging the group to assist him with the current threat to Khurrat. The group agrees to help, and Alannn teleports them to a hidden magic circle in the swamps. With a few hours of travel, the adventures arrive in Khurrat and are briefed by the Red Maiden. After fortifying the city and positioning the ally troops, the group rests for the incoming siege at dawn.
